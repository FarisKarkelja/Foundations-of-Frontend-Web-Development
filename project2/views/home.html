FAQ

How did your dance journey begin?

My dance journey began in Sarajevo, where I was born and raised. I was inspired by the vibrant local scene and started taking classes at a young age. My passion for dance grew as I participated in various local performances and eventually, international projects.

What are some common challenges dancers face?

Dancers often face physical challenges such as injuries and fatigue. Mentally, staying motivated and dealing with performance anxiety can be tough. Balancing practice with personal and professional commitments is another common challenge a dancer would face on a daily basis.

Do you offer dance lessons or workshops?

Yes, I offer both dance lessons and workshops. My lessons are designed for various skill levels, from beginners to advanced dancers. I also conduct workshops that focus on specific dance styles and techniques.

What is your preferred dance style?

While I enjoy many dance styles, my preferred style is Salsa. The energy, rhythm, and passion involved in Salsa dancing resonate with me deeply and allow me to express myself fully.

How to balance dance career with other commitments?

Balancing a dance career with other commitments requires good time management and setting priorities. I create a schedule that allocates time for practice, performances, and other responsibilities. Staying organized and setting clear goals helps maintain this balance.

What advice do you have for beginners in dance?

My advice for beginners is to stay patient and consistent. Practice regularly, even if it's just a little each day. Don't be afraid to make mistakes, as they are part of the learning process. Most importantly, enjoy the journey and have fun!

Can anyone learn to dance, regardless of age or experience?

Absolutely! Dance is for everyone, regardless of age or experience. It's never too late to start. With dedication and practice, anyone can learn to dance and enjoy the numerous benefits it brings.
